Game Rules

Racing Poker ™ Tournament is a game based on most of the rules of poker and played using a 52 card deck.

It contains six hands,every hand consists of five cards.

In first step, 30 random cards will be dealt and useless cards are thrown back into deck.

Useful cards and useless cards:

The useless cards will be decided based on below rules

To improve playing conditions Aces are always high.
A Pair is thrown in favour of a possible flush or double ended straight.
Hand Hierarchy determines the winner. Royal Flush, Straight Flush, Four of a kind, Full house, Flush, Straight.
Suit Hierachy is Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades.

After every deal, you will be given 30 seconds time to place your bet.

To place bets, please click on the hand, who you want to place bet and it opens a bet box.

Please check your current bets in current bets section in web page.

If you win your bet, it pays you the return amount on winning hand

You can observe your rank in the tournament in connected Player section

At the end of tournament, results pop up to indicate your position in tournament.

Leveling

The player level indicates the expertise of each player. Based on your rank in the tournament, you will get rewarded with a level. If you finish first in a tournament, you will raise / increase one level up. These are varied based on the number of times you make a profit in a tournament. If you make a profit twice, you will get rewarded with a ‘2shot’ bonus (up to a maximum of ‘10shot’).

Bonuses

These are varied based on number of times you make a profit in a tournament

If you make profit twice, you will get rewarded with a 2shot bonus and at maximum of 10shot bonus

2shot --- extra 10% of credits

3shot --- extra 20% of credits

4shot --- extra 30% credits, 1 bitlet

5shot --- extra 40% credits, 2 bitlets, 1 player level

6shot --- extra 50% credits, 3 bitlets , 1 player level

7shot --- extra 60% credits, 4 bitlets, 1 player level

8shot --- extra 70% credits, 5 bitlets, 1 player level

9shot --- extra 80% credits, 30 bitlets, 3 player levels

10shot --- extra 90% credits, 50 bitlets, 5 player levels
